Buyer’s Guide: Choosing Sleep Earbuds

  • Side sleeper fit: Look for low-profile shells.
  • Noise reduction: ANC for loud homes, passive seal for quiet rooms.
  • Battery: Minimum 6–8 hours single charge.
  • Comfort: Soft silicone or foam tips reduce pressure.
