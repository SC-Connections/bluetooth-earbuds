Buyer’s Guide: How to Choose Earbuds for Sleep

  • Side-sleeper comfort: low-profile shells prevent pillow pressure.
  • Noise control: ANC helps in louder homes; otherwise passive seal works.
  • Battery life: aim for 6–8 hours single-charge.
  • Controls: simple tap/hold to avoid accidental presses.
  • Fit options: foam tips or multiple sizes reduce pressure points.
